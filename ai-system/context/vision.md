# RGT Website - Vision

## What Is This
B2B service website for RGT Rakennuspalvelut Oy, a construction and demolition company operating in the Helsinki metropolitan area. The site is live at rgt.fi and serves as the company's primary digital presence and lead generation tool.

## Purpose
- Present RGT's construction and demolition services professionally to potential B2B and residential clients
- Generate inbound leads through contact form submissions
- Rank in Finnish search engines for construction/demolition service keywords in Helsinki area
- Provide structured data (JSON-LD) for rich search results

## Target Users
- Property managers and housing companies in Helsinki region
- Construction project managers looking for subcontractors
- Homeowners needing renovation or demolition services
- Anyone searching for "purkutyot helsinki", "rakennuspalvelut", "asbestipurku" etc.

## Current Status
MAINTENANCE MODE. The site is complete, deployed, and functioning. No new features are planned. Changes are limited to content updates, bug fixes, and SEO adjustments.

## Success Metrics
- Contact form submissions per month
- Organic search rankings for target keywords
- Page load performance (Core Web Vitals)
- Search engine indexing coverage across all service and area pages

## Language
Finnish only. No English version exists or is planned.
